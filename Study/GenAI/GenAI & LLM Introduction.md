**Generates New Content**
Generative AI refers to an AI that is able to generate new content ie. text, video, audio, code etc. It is a step-up from traditional ai which may be used for classification or object detection.

Examples of GenAI

- Text: ChatGPT
- Image/ Video: Dall-E
- Music/ Voice: TTS (Text to Speech)

## LLM (Large Language Model)

***History of AI Models***

- **Statistical Models** : Works on word frequency and predicts next word. Uses n-1 to predict n.
- **Recurring Neural Networks**: Similar to Statistical Model , but also had memory and more context.
- **Large Language Model** (Transformer Architecture):  Uses Transformer Architecture and works under self attention model. I predicts next word not on previous word basis but the whole context.
---
### Parameters
 In LLM Parameters refers to the total no of *weights and biases* across the entire neural network.
